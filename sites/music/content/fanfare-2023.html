Background

Chris composed this fanfare in 2020 in honor of the SpaceX Crew Dragon Demo launch: the first time in nearly a decade that American astronauts went to space on American rockets. In 2022, he revised it to record with the Budapest Scoring Orchestra, and he released it to the public in 2023.

You can find extended background and a history of the composition on Chris’ blog.
